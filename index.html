About Me

I am a second year undergraduate student at Northwestern University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in the McCormick School of Engineering. My interests lie primarily within human-centered design and front-end development. My goal is to contribute to the continuous effort of making the Internet and technology a user-friendly and intuitive space.
